School of Speed

Developed by Justin Henry, Kerry Peay, Paul Serb

Use the arrows keys to control Skatelyr, and dodge as many obstacles as you can to avoid getting caught by the angry mob behind you.
Collect sheets of paper on the ground and press Spacebar to shoot a spitball and destroy any obstacle it hits.

Cheat Codes:
Press the 1, 2, or 3 number key at any time to switch between levels.
